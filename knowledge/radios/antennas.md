# Antennas

## Summary

For Launch Canada 2026, the ground radio antenna setup is simple at the knowledge-base level: the control station uses Yagi antennas and the pad uses normal whip antennas.

## Planned LC 2026 Setup

| Location | Antenna Plan | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Control station (CS) | Yagi antennas | Directional antennas used from the human/operator side. |
| Pad | Normal whip antennas | Same general whip style used at URRG. |

## Context

The April 2026 range test compared multiple antenna setups over 5 km. Those results support confidence in the radio link, but the final high-level plan is to use Yagis at CS and whips at the pad.
